# jeffcopsc-Rproj
Data cleaning and analysis for JEFFCO PSC project.

Omni will provide evaluation services for the Jefferson County, Colorado Problem Solving Court (PSC) including overseeing all aspects of data collecting and reporting for the 1st Judicial District Recovery Court’s, Problem-Solving Court (PSC) as required by SAMHSA’s Center for Substance Abuse Treatment (CSAT) under the Government Performance and Results (GPRA) Modernization Act of 2010, to determine the impact of the PSC related to expansion of funding and services.
